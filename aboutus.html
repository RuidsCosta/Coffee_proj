Our History

Started in 2016, The Coffe House quickly gained clients to the point we were selling to all parts of the world. The goal is to serve and help people arround the globe to find and enjoy the brew that fits them the most.

The company traces its roots to a couple of friends, developers, that loved coffee but did not find the right one.
